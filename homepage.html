Updates on my life: Not that you asked

Here I will describe what I've been up to and hopefully get some cool ideas from you all on how to spend my time!










My name is Dagmawi Misgano. I'm currently a sophomore attending Zoom University studing Integrative Biology. I grew up in Seattle before spending 1.5 years on Zoom's cambridge campus and have now returned to Seattle indefinitley. I was in Cabot House but am now in my home. On campus, I am part of the many black student organizations such as the Black Men's Forum and the Black Students Association. I am also on board for the Minority Association of Pre-Medical Students and the Eritrean and Ethiopian Students Association club on campus. Hopefully you'll get a good sense of my life these days, and maybe you can give me suggestions too!
